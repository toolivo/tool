Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

Does it count words and characters accurately?

Yes. The counting system follows industry-standard rules used by professional text editors and online word counters.

How does the sentence counter work?

Sentences are detected using punctuation like . ! ? followed by a space or line break. The tool avoids false detection as much as possible.

Is there a limit to how much I can analyze?

You can analyze any length of text. Very large documents may take a moment to process depending on your device speed.

Can I clean and format my text before analyzing?

Yes. You can use the Toolivo Extra Space Remover to fix spacing issues or the Toolivo Case Converter to adjust text format before analyzing.

Do I need an account to use the Text Analyzer?

No. The tool is completely free to use and does not require registration, login or email.

Can I use Text Analyzer on mobile devices?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops without any issues.

Does Text Analyzer support multiple languages?

Currently, the tool is optimized for English text. However, it can count words, characters, and sentences in other languages as well, but accuracy may vary.

Can I analyze text from other tools like Google Docs?

Yes. Simply copy the text from Google Docs or any other platform and paste it into the Text Analyzer to get instant results.

How do I export or copy the analysis results?

You can use the copy button provided in the tool to copy all results to your clipboard. Some browsers may require HTTPS for clipboard access.

Troubleshooting

Counts look incorrect

Check if your settings include or exclude spaces and punctuation. These options change the results.

Tool feels slow

Large blocks of text may take longer to process on older devices. Refreshing the page may help.

Copy button not working

Some browsers block clipboard access unless the page is secure. Make sure the tool loads over HTTPS.
